# Reporting Issues

We understand that you may experience issues when using our software, and some of those may seem like a bug. Please do some research, read the manufacturer documentation for your synth, and ask questions in the appropriate per-emulator support channels before posting a formal bug report.

Please ensure you are using the most current version of the plugin; we do not retroactively provide fixes for older versions. You can find links in the announcements channel or in any of the emulator "support" channels in Pinned Messages.

Please ensure that you are able to replicate the issue; you are required to provide such steps in order for us to be able to assist. Many times, engaging in discussion within the support channels and providing a good explanation of your issue will result in a helpful user that can assist with an explanation or a resolution.

We do not directly provide support for third-party skins, please ensure any bugs you are showing are demonstrated and verified with the default skin provided with the emulator. Any reports for specific issues with third-party skins will be ignored and/or deleted.

## Bug Reports

To report a bug, use the following link:

**[Submit a Bug Report](https://tus.youtrack.cloud/form/f41165e1-01a0-416f-b8ac-5589b22e8ef5)**

A formal bug report will include the following information to describe your issue:

- **Emulator** - any specific emulator(s) you are able to duplicate the issue on.
- **Plugin Format** - any specific format(s) you are able to duplicate the issue on.
- **DAW** - any specific DAW(s) you are able to duplicate the issue on.
- **Operating System** - any specific OS(es) you are able to duplicate the issue on.
- **Summary** - this should be a clear and concise one-line Issue Description.
- **Description** - this is the area in which you will be expected to provide a more complete description of the issue with detailed steps to reproduce the issue. Include specific details regarding your environment such as DAW/OS/Plugin versions, CPU/RAM of your hardware, and any other environment-specific information. If we are unable to reproduce the issue using the steps you provide here, it may be difficult and time-consuming to address your issue.
- **Email address/Discord nickname** - provide a valid email address or your nickname from our Discord in a 'name@discord' format in the case that we need to contact you for further details or information. If you provide an email, any "public" comments for this ticket will be emailed to you by the system. There is no specific Discord feedback available at this time.

You may upload files to help demonstrate your issue; logs/crashdumps, screenshots, audio/video clips are examples of helpful information that we can use to better understand your issue. There is a 50MB limit for uploads.

CAPTCHA - you will be required to complete a CAPTCHA validation to help us minimize abuse of this system.
